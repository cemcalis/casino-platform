Reference Analysis

Betlesene: Conversion Density. Classic 3-column grid. Efficient data display.



Elitbahis: Luxury/Elite. Forest Green & Gold. Minimalist flat design for high-rollers.

Pragmatic Play / EGT: Live-Data Lobby. Hot/Cold indicators, 3D jackpot animations, 9:16 vertical video assets.

The "Neon Palace" Identity

Neon Palace will merge the Luxury of Elitbahis with the Live-Data Engagement of Pragmatic Play.





Palette: Deep Obsidian (#0A0A0B), Neon Electric Violet (#8B5CF6), and Champagne Gold (#D4AF37).



Typography: Inter for clarity, Space Grotesk for high-tech accents.



UX Goal: Reduce cognitive load through "Dark Space" while increasing FOMO via real-time win feeds and "Hot" game indicators.

Screen Roadmap





Lobby (Homepage): Dynamic hero with 3D jackpot, horizontal game categories, and "Live Wins" sidebar.



Slots Page: Pragmatic Play style grid with hover-play demos and volatility filters.



Live Casino: Immersive dark UI with real-time dealer status and bet minimums.



Sportsbook: Modern, clean layout focusing on live odds and 1-click betting.
